How the Vault Works

The Contest Vault lets commissioners collect entry fees without ever touching real money themselves. Here's the full flow, the fees, and what's different from a self-managed contest.

LeagueSafe Holds the Funds

Splash integrates directly with LeagueSafe. Funds are never held by Splash.

3% Processing Fee

A $100 payment sends $97 into the Vault and $3 to LeagueSafe for processing.

No Rake, No Service Fee

Enabling the Vault waives the standard Self Managed Service Fee — once a payment lands.

Available Everywhere

All 50 states plus Canada. No geolocation restrictions on collecting or paying.

Fee waiver note

The Self Managed Service Fee isn't waived automatically — it drops off only after at least one payment has actually landed in the Vault. Disable the Vault, and that fee comes right back. Expect stricter abuse-prevention rules around this waiver later in the year (targeted for August or the start of the season).

The one rule that matters most
